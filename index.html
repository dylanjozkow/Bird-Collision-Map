Bird Collision Map

This map shows all reported bird collisions from 2023 in Ottawa.

In Canada, bird collisions result in the death of 25 million birds per year.

Each marker displays the time of collision, species of bird and the status of the bird after the collision.

All collision data was found at birdmapper.org
